Through my dance school, I would like to my students to

Enjoy the process of learning the art form

Explore Explore the wealth of knowledge that the art form provides through its rich cultural history, theoretical aspects, technique involved in movement and expressions, rhythm framework and the nuances of story-telling.

Evolve as passionate artists, and develop a deeper understanding of the art form. I believe this is a cyclical process for any student of art as one journeys along the path of unravelling the beauty of the art form.

I provide opportunities for students to match these three key priorities so that they get a holistic learning experience.

Comprehensive Training: The syllabus covers everything from the basics to advanced techniques, theory, history of dance ensuring a deeper understanding of Bharatanatyam.

Flexible Learning: With online classes, I cater to students across the UK and India, making it convenient for everyone to join and learn.

Cultural Enrichment: Students can embrace the beauty of Indian classical dance, and its vast and rich cultural heritage through well-structured sessions, performances and workshops.

Join a vibrant community and experience the joy of Bharatanatyam.
