Through my dance school, I would like to my students to

  • ~ Enjoy the process of learning the art form
  • ~ Explore the wealth of knowledge that the art form provides through its rich cultural history, theoretical aspects, technique involved in movement and expressions, rhythm framework and the nuances of story-telling.
  • ~ Evolve as passionate artists, and develop a deeper understanding of the art form.

I believe this is a cyclical process for any student of art as one journeys along the path of unravelling the beauty of the art form.

I provide opportunities for students to match these three key priorities so that they get a holistic learning experience.

A few lines about me:

Born and raised in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India, my passion for dance started during my childhood. I am trained in two Indian classical dance forms, Bharatanatyam and Kuchipudi, and I have been pursuing the art form passionately for over 30 years. I run my own dance school, Krithika School of Dance which was started in London, and I have been training many young aspirants since 2012. I completed my post graduation in Bharatanatyam and also hold a master's degree in Business Administration.

I trained in Bharatanatyam with Smt. Hemalatha Ramachandran and Dr. S. Divyasena in Chennai. I had focussed training in Abhinaya from Smt. Kalanidhi Narayanan. I continue to train in Kuchipudi from Sri Madhavapeddi Murthy. I am also furthering my skills in Nattuvangam under the guidance of Sri M.Balachander, Mridangam artist and a resident teacher at The Bhavan, London.
